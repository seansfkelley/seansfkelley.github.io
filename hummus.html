Hummus Recipe

Units:  metric  •  stupid

One recipe makes approximately 1 liter1 quart of hummus.

Combine:

  • 1 clove of garlic (or 2 if you want a bite)
  • 530 grams3 cups drained chickpeas, liquid reserved
  • 60 grams¼ cup lemon juice
  • 60 grams¼ cup reserved chickpea liquid or water
  • 70 grams¼ cup tahini
  • 110 grams½ cup olive oil
  • ½ tsp (2 grams)½ tsp table salt

in a food processor. Process for 5 minutes, scraping down sides if necessary. Thin out with more chickpea liquid as desired.

Garnish with any or none of chopped parsley, paprika or sumac.
